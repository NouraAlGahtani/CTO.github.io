Operational Security

Future You

About You

An Operational Security (OpSec) Analyst focuses on protecting an organization’s sensitive information from unauthorized access and exploitation. They assess risks, implement security policies, and educate employees on safeguarding operational practices.

Skills and Qualifications
Educational Background
Bachelor’s degree in Cybersecurity, Information Technology, or a related field
Experience
Experience in security management, risk assessment, or a related field.
Technical Skills
Proficiency in risk management frameworks, security protocols, and incident response procedures. Familiarity with tools for monitoring and threat detection is essential.
Certifications
Relevant certifications (e.g., Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), Certified Information Security Manager (CISM), or CompTIA Security+) are a plus.
